## Placement of the new passage

**The Open Book and the Sealed Thunder (Rev 10:1–11)** is the trumpet-interlude — the mighty angel, the little open book, the seven thunders told to stay sealed, and the eating of the scroll. Place it **immediately after "The Trumpets — The Great Waking"** (canonically, chapter 10 sits between the sixth and seventh trumpets). In the site's current grouping that is inside the **"The Great Waking"** movement, right after the Trumpets passage. The full chapter text is in the manuscript file, ready to seed.

## Separate issue — order, not references (decide later)

These corrections fix *which verses each passage covers.* They do **not** resolve the larger question that the live passage **order/grouping differs from the reordered manuscript.** For example, the manuscript places "The Two Who Stand Together" and "The Beast Within" *before* the Throne, and "The Whole Earth Remembers" *after* Babylon (just before the Marriage), whereas the site groups them differently. That's an editorial ordering decision to settle on its own — keep it separate from this reference cleanup so the two don't get tangled.
